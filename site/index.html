Photo Competition Site

This website was made by Jason Hassold as a demonstration of my ability to make a well designed and fully functional website. This is a fake photo competition website where contestants can enter their photos to be voted on. Visitors can vote on their favorite photo so a winner can be named. The logo and tab icon where both designed by me using Adobe Illustrator. The photo of the camera was take by Markus Spiske and I found it on the website pexels.com, however the purple tint was added by me in Photoshop. The main CSS framework I used for this project was MaterializeCSS, which has a very modern and clean look. Other reasources were used in the making of this project, but those are the most significant.
